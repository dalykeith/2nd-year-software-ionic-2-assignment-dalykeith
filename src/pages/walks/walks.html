Barna Woods

Barna woods is located on the western boundary of Galway City and is owned and managed by Galway City Council. The woodland is part of a wider recreational amenity that includes Cappagh Park. The Barna Woods Project is a ‘Biodiversity Survey and Management Plan of Barna Woods’.

Renville Park

Trip advisor reviews: "family park for good walk and fun" "Renville park is about 5mins drive from oranmore and overlooks galway bay and sailing club. We go to Renville park fairly often with the family. Its a good park for a walk, run or just to relax. The paths have a good tarmac surface and there's a playground to motivate the kids to walk or run at a decent pace. Most weekends, there is a coffee and ice-cream vendor present. There is an exercise equipment area also. Outside the park, there is a lovely 4-5km walk by the sea with good paths. Myself and kids love to go through a few stones in the sea. Simple fun and good to Hurn off energy. Go along any time of year for some fresh air and exercise."
