John keoghs

John Keogh’s – The Lock Keeper Is a newly developed gastropub in the heart of Galway’s Westend. The pub has two bars and a private party room, all located on the ground floor. Renowned Head Chef Pat McEllin leads the kitchen team in producing the highest quality food, with the emphasis being on local and fresh. For this reason, the menu changes regularly. Everything is prepared in-house from scratch, from stocks and sauces to handmade ravioli to homemade desserts. All dishes are cooked to order to allow absolute freshness.

Mc Swiggans

For nearly 30 years McSwiggan’s has held a reputation as one of Galway’s finest eateries. Central to its success is its commitment to provide a high-quality dining experience matched with unrivalled customer service and a real passion for food! McSwiggans Interior A visit to McSwiggans gives you the opportunity to enjoy a relaxed breakfast, a leisurely coffee, a creamy pint, a fantastic meal, and great live music – all under one roof, with great friendly service, in a stylish and welcoming atmosphere! It’s no wonder that McSwiggan’s popularity keeps growing from strength to strength – if you haven’t before, why not come and enjoy our unique welcoming mix for yourself! If it’s your first visit, we’re sure it won’t be your last – we look forward to welcoming you back!

McGettigan’s

McGettigan’s Galway is located on Prospect Hill, just off Eyre Square and within steps of the bustling Shop Street area and many of the city’s most popular hotels and attractions such as the Spanish Arch, Galway Hooker Fountain and Lynch’s Castle. The venue opened in June 2015 and quickly became ranked highly on the list of Galway’s top restaurants and pubs in Galway. It boasts a very unique décor with a stunning raised bar sitting proudly in the middle of the ground floor, ‘The Library’ and garden area with indoor trees showcases perfectly, the attention to detail that goes into every McGettigan’s interior. The bar is open daily from 11am serving lunch, dinner and an extensive drinks menu including a range of carefully crafted signature cocktails and is also a great choice for a late bar in Galway.
